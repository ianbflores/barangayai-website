The Challenge

A Digital Divide That Can't Wait

The Philippines has 42,000+ barangays — the backbone of every Filipino community. Yet the vast majority have no access to AI tools, digital services, or the training needed to participate in the modern economy.

📡

No AI Access

Most barangay halls lack basic digital infrastructure, let alone AI-powered services that could transform how they serve citizens.

📚

Skills Gap

Without training and education, millions of Filipinos are at risk of being left behind as AI reshapes jobs, industries, and opportunities.

💼

Missed Economic Opportunity

AI-powered livelihoods, online work, and business growth remain out of reach for communities that have the talent but lack the tools.

Our Approach

AI for Every Filipino — In Every Community

BarangayAI is not just an app or a training program. It is a comprehensive ecosystem — combining education, technology, and government partnership — designed to work at the community level.

🎓

Education First

Practical, Filipino-language AI training programs for citizens, students, job seekers, and community leaders — at every skill level.

🛠️

Real Tools

The BarangayAI OS platform puts AI-powered services directly in the hands of barangay governments, businesses, and residents — no technical expertise needed.

🤝

Government Partnership

Working alongside LGUs, DICT, DOST, DILG, and TESDA to align AI deployment with national digital inclusion goals and local governance needs.

How It Works

Simple. Supported. Sustainable.

We designed BarangayAI for communities that need results — not lengthy procurement cycles or complicated onboarding. Three steps from inquiry to impact.

1

Assess

We work with your LGU to understand community needs, existing infrastructure, and pilot program goals. No cost, no commitment.

2

Deploy

We deploy the BarangayAI OS, customize it to your community's context, and train local facilitators to run programs independently.

3

Support

Ongoing technical support, updated AI tools, regular community reporting, and coordination with national government programs.
