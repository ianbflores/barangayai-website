Module 01

AI Assistant for Citizens

Now in Pilot

Our citizen-facing AI chatbot is currently live and serving residents via Facebook Messenger. Powered by Claude AI, it answers questions in Filipino 24/7 — about barangay documents, office hours, programs, and more. This is the foundation of the full BarangayAI platform.

Citizens can ask about document requirements, barangay schedules, local ordinances, health information, and more — and get accurate, helpful responses instantly, without waiting in line or calling the barangay hall.

✦ Key Features
🇵🇭 Filipino language support
📱 Messenger & web access
🕐 24/7 availability
📋 Service request routing
🔄 Live escalation to staff
📊 Conversation analytics

Module 08

Agriculture & Rural Support Tools

Coming 2026

Designed for communities where agriculture and fishing are the primary livelihoods, this module brings practical AI guidance directly to the hands of farmers and fisherfolk — in their language, on the tools they already use.

Integrated with Philippine weather services, market price data, and agricultural databases, the Agriculture module provides context-specific advice that accounts for local crop varieties, regional conditions, and seasonal factors.

✦ Key Features
🌱 Crop advisory AI
🐛 Photo pest identification
🌦️ PAGASA weather integration
💹 Market price tracker
🐟 Fishing condition alerts
📱 Works on basic smartphones
Technical Details

Built to Work Everywhere in the Philippines

The BarangayAI OS is designed for Philippine conditions — low-bandwidth environments, mixed devices, limited IT support, and the full diversity of 42,000+ communities.

Access Methods
Web browser, Facebook Messenger, SMS
Language Support
Filipino, English, regional dialects
Device Compatibility

Connectivity
Optimized for low-bandwidth connections

Zero — fully managed by BarangayAI
