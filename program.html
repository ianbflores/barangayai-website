Pillar 01 Education & Literacy
AI Education for Citizens

Empowering Every Filipino with AI Knowledge

AI literacy is the foundation of everything BarangayAI does. Before tools can help a community, that community needs to understand what AI is, how it works, and how to use it safely and confidently. Our education programs are built in Filipino, designed for every skill level, and delivered in formats that work — whether online, offline, or at the barangay hall.

We believe that AI education is not a privilege for those in Metro Manila with university degrees. Every tricycle driver, every market vendor, every OFW's family member deserves to understand the technology shaping their future.

Who Benefits

Students Job Seekers Freelancers OFW Families Seniors & Lifelong Learners Community Leaders

What We Teach

From "What is AI?" for complete beginners to prompt engineering, AI tool use for work, and digital safety — our curriculum meets learners where they are and takes them as far as they want to go.

✦ Key Outcomes
  • Improved digital and AI literacy across all age groups
  • Better employment opportunities through in-demand skills
  • Increased confidence in using technology for daily life
  • Reduced vulnerability to online scams and misinformation
  • Stronger foundation for advanced AI livelihood training

Pillar 02 Economic Empowerment
AI Livelihood & Economic Opportunities

New Income Streams for Every Filipino

One of the most immediate ways AI can transform a barangay is by creating real, sustainable income for its residents. The global demand for AI-powered services — content creation, virtual assistance, data processing, social media management — has never been higher, and the Philippines already has the talent, the English proficiency, and the drive to lead.

BarangayAI's livelihood pillar gives residents the skills, the tools, and the connections to access that demand — from their home, at flexible hours, on their own terms.

Who Benefits

Stay-at-Home Parents Returning OFWs Fresh Graduates Unemployed Residents Micro-Entrepreneurs
✦ Key Outcomes
  • New home-based income opportunities for barangay residents
  • Access to the global AI services freelance market
  • Reduced need for residents to migrate for work
  • Stronger local economic circulation
  • Community-wide increase in digital income capacity
Pillar 03 Small Business Growth
AI for Small Businesses

Helping Local Businesses Grow, Compete, and Thrive

The sari-sari store. The carinderia. The local tailor, the market vendor, the home baker. These small businesses are the economic heartbeat of every barangay — and they deserve access to the same AI tools that large corporations use to market themselves, serve customers, and operate efficiently.

BarangayAI's business pillar makes enterprise-level AI tools accessible and affordable for the smallest Filipino businesses — with training, templates, and ongoing support.

Who Benefits

Sari-Sari Stores Carinderias Market Vendors Service Providers MSMEs Home-Based Businesses

AI Tools for Business

Marketing automation, social media content generation, AI customer support, business planning assistants, inventory guidance, and competitive analysis — all made accessible for businesses with limited budgets and technical resources.

✦ Key Outcomes
  • Increased sales through better marketing and online visibility
  • Reduced costs through operational automation
  • Improved customer service and satisfaction
  • Better business planning and financial decision-making
  • Stronger ability to compete with larger businesses

Pillar 04 Government Modernization
AI for Barangay Government

Modernizing Public Service, One Barangay at a Time

Barangay officials are often overwhelmed — serving hundreds or thousands of residents with minimal staff, aging systems, and mountains of paperwork. AI can transform this reality without replacing anyone. It can automate repetitive tasks, answer routine citizen inquiries, generate documents, and give officials the information they need to make better decisions for their community.

BarangayAI works directly with barangay captains and kagawads to implement AI in a way that respects their existing workflows and builds their confidence as technology leaders.

Who Benefits

Barangay Captains Kagawads Barangay Secretaries Barangay Treasurers Residents Seeking Services
✦ Key Outcomes
  • Faster and more efficient public service delivery
  • Significantly reduced administrative workload
  • Better citizen engagement and satisfaction
  • More transparent and accountable governance
  • Compliance with national digital governance mandates
Pillar 05 Agriculture & Fisheries
AI for Farmers & Fisherfolk

Bringing AI Intelligence to Every Farm and Fishing Boat

The Philippines is an agricultural nation, yet most farmers and fisherfolk make decisions the same way their grandparents did — based on tradition, intuition, and luck. AI can give them something their grandparents never had: real-time data, predictive insights, and intelligent recommendations that improve every decision they make.

BarangayAI's agriculture pillar uses AI to level the playing field for the millions of Filipino farmers and fisherfolk who deserve better tools, better prices, and better futures.

Who Benefits

Rice & Corn Farmers Vegetable Growers Fisherfolk Agricultural Cooperatives Rural Communities
✦ Key Outcomes
  • Improved crop yields through better planning and pest management
  • Reduced post-harvest losses through AI-guided decisions
  • Better market prices through real-time pricing intelligence
  • Increased income for farm households
  • More resilient agriculture in the face of climate change

Pillar 06 Community Health
AI for Community Health & Wellness

Healthier Communities Through Accessible AI

In many Philippine barangays, access to reliable health information is a luxury. Residents may live hours from a hospital, have limited access to doctors, or face barriers of language and literacy. AI cannot replace doctors — but it can make health information more accessible, help communities understand preventive care, and empower health workers to serve more people more effectively.

BarangayAI's health pillar is built with safety and responsibility at its core — always directing users to professional care when needed, while supporting informed, healthier daily decisions.

Who Benefits

Families Mothers & Children Elderly Residents Barangay Health Workers Community Clinics
✦ Key Outcomes
  • Better health awareness and preventive care practices
  • Improved family wellness through accessible information
  • Empowered community health workers serving more residents
  • Stronger community resilience against health challenges
  • Reduced health information gaps in underserved areas
