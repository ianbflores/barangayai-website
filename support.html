Why It Matters

The Philippines Needs This — Now

AI is advancing faster than most governments can respond. The communities most likely to be left behind are also the most numerous — the 42,000+ barangays where millions of Filipinos live, work, and raise their families.

Without deliberate, funded intervention, the AI revolution will widen — not close — the gap between urban elites and rural communities. BarangayAI exists to prevent that outcome.

Your support does not fund overhead. It funds programs — training sessions, platform access, facilitator salaries, and community outreach that directly reaches Filipinos who would otherwise have no access to AI.

Where Every Peso Goes

We believe donors deserve to know exactly how their contributions are used. Here's how BarangayAI allocates funding across our four core investment areas:

🎓 Training Programs & Facilitators45%

Paying community AI facilitators, developing curriculum, running workshops, and certifying trainers.

🖥️ Technology & Platform Access30%

Deploying and maintaining the BarangayAI OS, API costs, hosting, and device support for communities.

📣 Community Outreach15%

Reaching and enrolling residents, local events, barangay coordination, and awareness campaigns.

⚙️ Operations & Administration10%

Core team salaries, legal compliance, reporting, and organizational infrastructure.
